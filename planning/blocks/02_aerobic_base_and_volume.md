# Block 2: Aerobic Base And Volume Consolidation

## Dates

- `2026-06-22` to `2026-08-16`
- Duration: `8 weeks`

## Block Objective

- Improve aerobic engine and durability.
- Raise the amount of quality work the body can absorb later.

## Primary Stimulus

- More total running time.
- Longer easy runs and long runs.
- Controlled steady work under threshold.

## Weekly Volume Guide

- Typical range: `40-55 km/week`.
- Possible upper edge: `60 km/week` only if recovery and shin status are good.

## Key Session Types

- Aerobic steady runs by heart rate.
- Progression runs finishing in upper aerobic zones.
- Long runs with controlled fast finish only when fresh.
- Short tempo fragments that do not depend on a fixed threshold pace yet.

## Practical Rules

- One main quality day and one secondary aerobic stimulus most weeks.
- Use this block to learn which easy pace corresponds to stable heart rate.
- Introduce terrain variation, but be careful with sudden downhill load.

## Success Criteria

- Higher volume with stable recovery.
- Easy pace naturally improves at similar heart rate.
- Long run becomes a reliable weekly anchor.

## Failure Triggers

- Persistent fatigue or heavy legs.
- Shin starts reacting to volume more than to speed.
- Recovery days stop feeling easy.
