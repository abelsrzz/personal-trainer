# Block 6: Taper And Race Execution

## Dates

- `2027-01-25` to `2027-02-06`
- Duration: `2 weeks`

## Block Objective

- Preserve sharpness while removing fatigue.
- Arrive fresh, healthy and clear on pacing.

## Primary Stimulus

- Reduced volume.
- Small doses of race-specific rhythm.
- High recovery quality.

## Weekly Volume Guide

- Reduce volume but keep some intensity.
- Avoid sudden inactivity that makes the legs feel flat.

## Key Session Types

- Short race-pace reminders.
- Light neuromuscular work.
- Easy runs with strict recovery control.

## Practical Rules

- No heroic sessions.
- Prioritize sleep, routine and logistics.
- Confirm pacing strategy, warm-up and race shoes.

## Success Criteria

- Fresh legs.
- Stable shin.
- Confidence in the final strategy.

## Failure Triggers

- Last-minute hard training.
- Panic adjustments after one bad session.
- Any avoidable shin flare in race week.
