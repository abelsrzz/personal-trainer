# Goal Gates For 35:00

## Purpose

- Keep `35:00` alive as an ambition without letting it distort training too early.
- Convert the final target into measurable checkpoints.
- Force recalibration if the body or data disagree.

## Gates

1. Base estable
   - Average last 4 weeks at least `40 km/week`.
   - Long run at least `14 km`.
   - No high-risk reviews in the last 28 days.
   - Shin pain no higher than `2/10`.

2. Umbral competitivo
   - Recent 5k or equivalent evidence around `19:00` or better.
   - Threshold work repeatable without racing every repetition.

3. Precondicion 35:00
   - Average last 4 weeks at least `50 km/week`.
   - Long run at least `16 km`.
   - Recent 5k around `18:00` or better.
   - No high-risk reviews or shin warning trend.

4. Seleccion 35:00
   - Recent 5k around `17:15` or better, or equivalent tune-up evidence.
   - Recent 10k/tune-up evidence around `36:30` or better.
   - Repeatable 10k-specific sessions without excessive fatigue.

## Current Rule

- Until Gate 4 is close, `3:30/km` is not a normal training pace.
- If only Gates 1-2 are passed, the plan develops the athlete and keeps the goal aspirational.
- If Gate 3 is passed, the goal remains aggressive but plausible.
- If Gate 4 is passed, `35:00` can be selected as race strategy.
