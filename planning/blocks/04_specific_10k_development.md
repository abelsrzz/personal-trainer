# Block 4: Specific 10k Development

## Dates

- `2026-10-12` to `2026-12-20`
- Duration: `10 weeks`

## Block Objective

- Convert the aerobic and threshold base into 10k-specific performance.
- Improve speed endurance, rhythm changes and race economy.

## Primary Stimulus

- 10k-oriented intervals.
- Longer repetitions close to target race demands.
- Controlled race-pace exposure.

## Weekly Volume Guide

- Typical range: `50-60 km/week`, adjusted to recovery.

## Key Session Types

- Repetitions such as 1k, 1200 m, 1600 m or 2k at controlled 10k-oriented effort.
- Mixed sessions combining threshold and race-pace segments.
- Long run with moderate quality only when weekly recovery is good.
- Occasional test races or time trials if useful.

## Practical Rules

- Do not force the final dream pace too early.
- Use this block to define realistic pace bands for Block 5.
- Favor flat or gently rolling routes similar to the `S` race profile.

## Success Criteria

- Specific sessions become more repeatable.
- Current 10k pace clearly improves from the initial 4:22/km reference.
- The final target range becomes evidence-based.

## Failure Triggers

- Repeated inability to recover from specific work.
- Shin reacts to faster turnover.
- Weekly plan starts revolving around surviving sessions instead of adapting to them.
