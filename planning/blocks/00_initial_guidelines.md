# Initial Planning Guidelines

## Main Constraint

- Protect the left shin and avoid fast load spikes.

## Current Objective

- Rebuild consistent training from a 30-40 km/week base.
- Improve general sensations before aggressive race-specific work.

## Practical Consequences

- Prioritize controlled aerobic work.
- Use only two quality slots at most, matching Tuesday and Thursday preferences.
- Keep the long run on Sunday when possible.
- Use heart rate as the main control tool outside interval and tempo sessions.
- Be careful with aggressive plated shoes while shin discomfort is active.
