# Block 3: Threshold Development And Strength Endurance

## Dates

- `2026-08-24` to `2026-10-18`
- Duration: `8 weeks`

## Block Objective

- Lift sustainable speed before true 10k-specific work.
- Develop the ability to hold controlled quality without large lactate spikes.
- Estimate threshold pace from repeatable sessions and/or a checkpoint race.

## Primary Stimulus

- Cruise intervals.
- Longer tempo segments.
- Controlled hill or rolling-terrain strength endurance.
- Continued aerobic volume support.

## Weekly Volume Guide

- Typical range: `45-60 km/week`.
- Optional upper edge: `60-62 km/week` only if the previous block was stable and the shin is quiet.
- Keep at least one lighter week every `3-4 weeks`.

## Key Session Types

- Cruise intervals such as `5-6 x 1000 m`, `4 x 1500 m` or `3-4 x 2000 m` at controlled threshold effort.
- Sustained tempo such as `2 x 12-15 min`, `3 x 10 min` or `20-30 min` continuous when calibrated.
- Rolling aerobic runs for strength endurance, avoiding aggressive downhills.
- Short relaxed strides after easy runs.

## Practical Rules

- Threshold should feel controlled, not like repeated race efforts.
- Pace prescriptions come from current checkpoint data, heart-rate trend and RPE.
- If shin load rises with hills, move back to flat threshold work.
- Do not chase `3:30/km`; this block is about raising the floor.

## Checkpoints

- Late September or early October: controlled 5k, 8k, 20-minute effort or equivalent session.
- End of block: define a realistic threshold band and a provisional 10k band for Block 4.
- If 5k ability is still far from sub-19 shape, `35:00` remains aspirational and must not drive workouts.

## Success Criteria

- Better control in sustained quality sessions.
- Clearer estimate of threshold pace and realistic 10k pace band.
- More comfort running near current 10k effort.
- Ability to recover reliably between Tuesday and Thursday stimuli.

## Failure Triggers

- Sessions require racing effort to complete.
- Downhill or hill work irritates the shin.
- Recovery between quality days becomes unreliable.
- Easy runs trend slower with higher HR for more than one week.
