# Block 3: Threshold Development And Strength Endurance

## Dates

- `2026-08-17` to `2026-10-11`
- Duration: `8 weeks`

## Block Objective

- Lift sustainable speed.
- Develop the ability to hold quality work without large lactate spikes.

## Primary Stimulus

- Cruise intervals.
- Longer tempo segments.
- Strength endurance through rolling terrain or controlled hill work.

## Weekly Volume Guide

- Typical range: `45-60 km/week`.

## Key Session Types

- Threshold development sessions based on feel, heart rate trend and later calibration.
- 1k to 2k repetitions at controlled effort.
- Sustained moderate tempos.
- Hill sessions that build force without sprinting.

## Practical Rules

- This block should help estimate threshold pace from training data.
- Quality should feel controlled, not desperate.
- If shin load rises with hills, move back to flat threshold work.

## Success Criteria

- Better control in sustained quality sessions.
- Clearer estimate of threshold pace and realistic 10k pace band.
- More comfort running near current 10k effort.

## Failure Triggers

- Sessions require racing effort to complete.
- Downhill or hill work irritates the shin.
- Recovery between quality days becomes unreliable.
