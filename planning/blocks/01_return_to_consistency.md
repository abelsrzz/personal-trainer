# Block 1: Return To Consistency And Tissue Tolerance

## Dates

- `2026-05-25` to `2026-06-21`
- Duration: `4 weeks`

## Block Objective

- Establish a stable routine without aggravating the left shin.
- Build consistency before chasing specific performance.

## Primary Stimulus

- Low-risk aerobic volume.
- Gradual exposure to controlled faster running.
- Better tissue tolerance through repeatable load.

## Weekly Volume Guide

- Start from `30-40 km/week`.
- Progress only toward `35-45 km/week` if symptoms allow.

## Key Session Types

- Easy runs controlled by heart rate.
- Short strides on good days.
- Light hill sprints only if the shin is quiet.
- Controlled fartlek or short cruise intervals well below all-out effort.
- Sunday long run kept comfortable.

## Practical Rules

- Maximum two quality touches per week, with one of them possibly being very light.
- Avoid back-to-back hard days.
- Prioritize forgiving shoes for most mileage.
- Limit use of aggressive plated shoes while symptoms are active.

## Success Criteria

- Four consistent weeks.
- No worsening of shin discomfort.
- Improved general sensations.
- Long run and total mileage feel more routine.

## Failure Triggers

- Rising shin pain during or after easy runs.
- Need to force pace to stay in planned zones.
- General fatigue worsening for more than one week.
